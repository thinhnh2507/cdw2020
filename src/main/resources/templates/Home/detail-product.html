RESPONSIVE RUNNING SHOES FEATURING DISNEY'S GOOFY.

Join Disney's Goofy as he tries to learn new sports in these adidas Ultraboost Running Shoes. Responsive cushioning returns energy to your stride with every footstrike for that I-could-run-forever feeling.

  • Adaptive running shoes
  • Responsive Boost midsole
